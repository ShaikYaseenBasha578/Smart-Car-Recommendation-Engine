How CarRec Works

CarRec turns plain-English requests into filters, reads intent with an ML parser, and ranks matching cars based on the data available in the project dataset. Used-car search also aggregates listings from supported external sources as a prototype.

Natural Language Search

Describe the car you want by budget, body style, fuel type, transmission, features, or use case.

Smart Filtering & Ranking

The app parses your intent, applies filters, and ranks recommendations with match reasons where data supports them.

New + Used Car Search

Explore new-car recommendations from the processed dataset and try used-car aggregation from supported sources.
